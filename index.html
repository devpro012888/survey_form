Welcome to the Coast Guard Yeoman Customer Service Survey

Your feedback is essential to helping us improve the administrative support services by our Yeoman staff. This short survey is designed to assess the quality, efficiency, and professionalism of the assistance you received.

Whether you visted for pay and entitlements, travel, orders, evaluations, or any other personnel-relted matter ,your input helps us identify strengths, address concerns, and enhance the overall customer experience.

The survey is anonymous unless you choosse to provide identifying information, and should take no more than 5 mintures to complete. Thank you for taking the time to share your thoughts and help us better serve you and the Coast Guard community.
